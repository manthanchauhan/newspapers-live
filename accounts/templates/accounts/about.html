    What do we do?

    Newspapers is service which allows you to mark your newspaper as present or absent each day, so that when your newspaper vendor comes for payment, you don't need to calculate the bill yourself.

    How to get started?

    Follow the steps to enjoy this amazing service.

    Step 1: Signup

    To signup, fill this really small form.

    Step 2: Plan Upload

    Signed up? cool. Now go to this page and upload your weekly newspaper plan.
    Now you are all set to automate your newspaper billings forever :).

    Step 3: Starting Session

    We call billing cycle a session. To start a session, go to the home page and click "Start new session". Enter the date from which your newspaper billing starts, and press "Start Session".
    Great! you have started your session. You are now ready to mark any date as absent whenever you miss your newspaper.

    Step 4: Marking absentees

    Whenever you do not get your newspaper, go to your home page and tap the date to change its status, marking it absent.
